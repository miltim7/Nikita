Rules for creating templates

To create a new template, you need to download, fill out and send an application to names@nikita.kg

Application form for approval of transactional / service SMS templates

Designations:

Sign "X" in Latin means any one character

Sign “~~” in Latin denotes a sequence of any characters

Examples:

1. Your login code: XXX

2. Karta “XXX”: servis informirovaniya podkluchen.

3. Vash zakaz ID ~~ obrabotan na summu ~~ som, data vydachi XX.XX.XX

Rules for assigning templates to traffic types

Transactional SMS:

TO Transactional include SMS messages informing the Subscriber about the execution of cash flow operations on his account or about the impossibility of performing such an operation.

Under funds movement operation are understood:

  • completed operation of crediting/receipt/withdrawal of funds reserve;
  • completed transaction of write-off/expense/transfer/reservation of funds;
  • payment of commission, accrual of interest on the deposit, conversion of funds into another currency;
  • cancellation or impossibility of performing the listed operations.
  • The Transaction Message Template must EXPLICITLY indicate the type of transaction, account number, and amount.

Service SMS:

A service message is considered to be a message informing the Subscriber about the status of the provision of service/customer services to him/her.

Under status of service provision is understood:

  • One-time password (OTP);
  • Welcome message upon registration in the service;
  • Notifying the user about events affecting the terms of service provision (change of telephone number, provision of service, for example, increase in subscription fee);
  • Order/purchase confirmation;
  • Product delivery/return/exchange status;
  • Reminders about a planned event as part of the provision of a service (for example, about an appointment);
  • Informing about technical work/warnings about suspension/resumption of services;
  • Notifications about entry/exit/attempts to enter the customer self-service system;
  • Information about class schedules, current and final achievements, messages about internal routine or homework and other messages sent by educational institutions that do not contradict the rules for classifying Templates as Service;
  • Informing about the client’s debt and details of the agreement on the basis of which the client’s debt arose;
  • Informing about the conditions and repayment schedule of the client’s debt;
  • Informing the client about accruals/debits/expiration dates for points/coupon redemption in loyalty programs
  • Other Messages corresponding to the definition specified in clause 4.1 of these Special Conditions

Promotional SMS:

  • All Messages that are not Transactional or Service Messages are considered Promotional Messages. At the same time, the collection of Templates for advertising traffic is not provided.
  • If the SMS text contains extra spaces at the beginning and/or end of the text, such SMS will be recognized by the platform as NOT matching the Template.
  • All National A2P SMS that do not comply with the Templates, as well as all cases of sending Service/Advertising/Transactional SMS messages in any mixed combination will also be classified as Advertising messages.
