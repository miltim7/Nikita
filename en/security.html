legality

When choosing an SMS service provider, you cannot rely only on the proposed cost of SMS messages.
After all, by sending SMS messages you trust your partner with the contacts of your clients!
In addition, cheap SMS gateways are not always able to provide decent quality of service and full functionality.

Yes, having our own SMS platform we can truly offer low prices for SMS mailings, however, we advise you to also pay attention to our other advantages.

By carrying out any mass mailings, you and I carry out activities strictly regulated by several laws of the Kyrgyz Republic, including the law on advertising and the law on communications, as well as the standards and requirements of cellular operators.

We make every effort to act within the law.

We have the necessary license for Telematic communication services, we work with contractors only under official contracts that reflect all legal requirements. We fight SPAM and fraud, i.e. We produce only the so-called "Address mailings".

And that is why for our clients we open the function of replacing the sender’s number only if there is a signed agreement.
