Banks, MFOs and Services (Big business)

Task: Security and notifications.

Scenario:

  • Newsletter:Sending one-time passwords (OTP) to log into your personal account or confirm a transaction.
  • Tool:Direct API channel with priority delivery (up to 5-10 seconds).
