National and International sms

Sending SMS messages from names that operators consider national and register will be charged at the rates for national sender names. All other unregistered names will be considered international, and SMS messages from such names will be charged at the rates for international sender names.

International ones include alphanumeric names that are used by international brands, payment systems, international electronic wallets and/or payment systems (for example, WebMoney, Qiwi Wallet, etc.), international social networks, international gaming Internet services, international email services, file hosting services, search engines (for example, Mail.Ru, Yahoo, Rambler.ru, Google, etc.).

Download and fill out the application form for approval of the sender’s name Learn more about international sender names

Registering sender names

Regardless of type, national or international, all sender names must be registered.

Only after registering a name with each operator will it be possible to send SMS messages to subscribers of a given operator from the registered name.

To register the sender's name, you must fill out an application and send a request by e-mail names@nikita.kg.

The registration period for new names for clients with national traffic takes up to 10 working days.

Tariffing features

Billing for sent SMS messages depends on the established tariff plan, the name of the sender, and the number of message parts. Incorrect shipments are not charged.

At the operator Beeline (Sky Mobile LLC) SMS messages are also charged depending on the type of messages, which are divided into service, transactional and advertising. Distribution is carried out according to transactional/service message templates previously agreed with the operator.

Transactional (banking) SMS:

Transactional messages include SMS messages informing the Subscriber about transactions involving the movement of funds on his account or about the impossibility of performing such an operation. The message template must explicitly indicate the transaction type, account number and amount.

Service SMS:

A service message is a Message that informs the Subscriber about the status of the client’s service/service, as well as informing about events and/or operations within the framework of the services provided.

Promotional SMS:

Advertising SMS are considered all messages that do not fall under the templates of service and transactional SMS.

Tariff for financial institutions

With the operator MegaCom (CJSC Alfa Telecom), messages sent by financial institutions are paid at a separate rate. The list of sender names for organizations providing financial services (banks, microcredit companies, pawnshops) is established by the operator.

What is a one-time password?

One Time Password (OTP) – a simple and reliable form of two-factor authentication.

Two-factor authentication is additional level of security, allowing you to eliminate the vulnerabilities of the standard password and is necessary to protect your company and its data.

Protect your service and users from unauthorized actions.

Verify clients and their actions using one-time SMS codes.

A one-time SMS code is an excellent solution for companies whose business is directly related to the Internet and working with a large client base.

How does client authorization occur?

Two-step verification is based on phone number verification. This is a reliable and fail-safe method that will work 99% of the time.

It is clear and does not require the user to install additional software, purchase special devices, or perform complex actions.

  1. The user enters their phone number
  2. Our platform sends him an SMS with a one-time password
  3. The user receives an SMS message with a password, after which he enters it on the website or application.

Payment methods

Regardless of the form of your organization (individual, legal entity or private enterprise), you can top up your account in our system using any of the following methods:

Bank cards

Topping up with a bank card is the easiest and most profitable way to top up.

We accept cards from all the most common payment systems:

To top up your card, go to your personal account on our website (Home → top up account)
Select the appropriate type of replenishment, indicate the replenishment amount and follow further instructions from the system.

Commission when topping up with a card – 0%

Cashless payment

Recommended (but not required) payment method for legal entities and private individuals.

Payment is made by bank transfer against the account. To issue an invoice you need:

  1. Register and enter into a written agreement with Nikita Online LLC
  2. Place an online order to top up your account in your personal account for the desired amount
    (Personal account → Home → Top up account)

The invoicing process takes a few seconds without the manager's participation.

You immediately receive a scanned copy of the certified document.

The original document is delivered separately by regular mail.

Maybe accelerated enrollment funds to your account in the system upon provision of a scan of a certified payment order (for clients who have entered into an agreement).

In the purpose of payment, you must indicate the account number and the phrase “for SMS notification services.”

Payment via Mbank

Quick account replenishment through the Mbank mobile application.

To top up your account via Mbank:

  1. Open the Mbank application
  2. Go to the "Payments" section
  3. Select recipient "NIKITA"
  4. Specify the replenishment amount
  5. Confirm payment

Commission: to be confirmed with the bank

Funds are credited to your account within a few minutes.

Payment via ELQR

A modern payment method through the ELQR system.

ELQR is a unified system of fast payments in Kyrgyzstan.

To top up your account via ELQR:

  1. Open the mobile application of your bank or payment service
  2. Find the option to pay by QR code
  3. Scan the NIKITA QR code or use the recipient's details
  4. Enter the amount and confirm payment

Commission: from 0%

Funds are credited instantly.

Payment via Pay24 terminals

When making a payment at the terminal you will need:

  1. Select section Other services → Programs and electronic services, find in the list of services SMS mailings Nikita Online
  2. Indicate the phone number in international format that you specified when registering on our website

Commission when paying through the Pay24 terminal – 2%

You can also make payments from the "Mobile Wallet" on the website pay24.asia.

No commission is charged.

Payment via ELSOM electronic wallet

The ELSOM electronic wallet offers you a convenient and simple way to pay for services anywhere and at any time, in several different ways, subject to the availability of a mobile network or the Internet.

  1. Payment using USSD commands (available for Beeline, MegaCom subscribers):
    • Dial the command on your mobile phone *900#.
    • In the menu that appears, select "Payments/Commercial Services" → "Pay Bill".
    • Then select the "Other" category and find the item in the list of suppliers "Nikita Online".
    • Enter personal account (phone number in international format that you specified when registering on our website), indicate the payment amount and confirm payment;
  2. Payment using the ELSOM mobile application:
    • Download and install the ELSOM mobile application from the website www.elsom.kg (available for IOS and Android platforms).
    • When paying through a mobile application, you must enter the section "Other services" and select from a list of suppliers “Nikita Online SMS mailings”.
    • As details, please provide the phone number in international format that you provided when registering on our website.
  3. Payment through a personal wallet on the ELSOM website:
    • Use your personal wallet on the site www.elsom.kg
    • When paying through a personal wallet, you must enter the section "Other services" and select from a list of suppliers “Nikita Online SMS mailings”.
    • As details, please provide the phone number in international format that you provided when registering on our website.

There is no commission when paying through the ELSOM mobile wallet.
